The Ship's Log

A diary by Capt. Crit McGillicutty

By Crit McGillicutty
Island Breeze Tribune

A terrible danger!

The beast was out of control! Soon enough, my maties and I had figured the end times were nigh! I gathered our loot and booty, and drawn from the inspiration of the great Captain Jack Sparrow, I jumped ship and abandoned my mates! For there was nothin’ to be done!
